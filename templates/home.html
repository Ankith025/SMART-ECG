About Cardiac

Cardiac arrhythmia is an irregular heartbeat disrupting normal heart rhythm. Early detection via ECG ensures timely intervention through clinical or lifestyle management.

Algorithm

SMART ECG leverages the Weighted KNN algorithm for accurate arrhythmia classification. Data proximity weighting optimizes predictive performance across diverse ECG samples.

Efficiency

Our ML-based system achieves 94% accuracy in arrhythmia detection, ensuring reliable real-time cardiac monitoring and decision support.

About ECG

Trained on over 1300 ECG samples from the Kaggle Cardiac Arrhythmia dataset, our system provides efficient signal classification and visualization for clinicians and researchers.
